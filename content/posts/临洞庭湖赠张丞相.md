## 临洞庭湖赠张丞相

**孟浩然**

八月湖水平，涵虚混太清。

气蒸云梦泽，波撼岳阳城。

欲济无舟楫，端居耻圣明。

坐观垂钓者，徒有羡鱼情。

## A Poem on Dongting Lake

*Meng Haoran, tr.*

In August the lake is level with the land,
its vast waters blending into empty sky.
Steam rises from the Cloud-Dream marshes,
the waves shake the walls of Yueyang.

I long to cross, but have no oar, no guide;
To idle here shames me before this age.
I watch the fishermen work the other side —
And ache for their catch, I who have no stage.
